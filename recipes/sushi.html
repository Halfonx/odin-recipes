Sushi Recipe

Description

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice, nori and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • A cucumber
  • An avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice
  2. Mix it with the rice vinegar
  3. Cut the salmon, avocado and cucumber
  4. Assemble the sushi rolls
  5. Roll the sushi tightly and cut it in pieces
